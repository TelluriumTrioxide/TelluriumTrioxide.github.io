In short

My name is Teo Silfverhjelm and I am a video game programmer, currently working at Liquid Swords in Stockholm, Sweden. I specialize in game oriented AI and gameplay systems, communication and leadership, as well as learning and adapting new techniques quickly.

All my life I have been playing games. I vividly remember the first time I got a console of my own – the Gameboy Advance SP, at the age of six, with the very nostalgic Pokémon Fire Red.
Ever since, games have been a big part of my life.

Interests

Beyond programming and playing video games, I love everything medieval. The sweetspot is around the 14th-15th century, at the height of the Holy Roman Empire. But earlier and later are both interesting periods, and the later renaissance is quite interesting as well!

I am a frequent crafter of things, doing a little bit of anything that comes to mind in a flash of inspiration. Whether it be crocheting, wire crafting, making music, sewing, or making stupid memes in photoshop, there's always some project in progress. But it's quite random, and has to have some *reason* for me to get hyperfocused on it.

Curretly I'm neck deep in Kingdom Come: Deliverance 2, and I might daresay it (or it's predecessor) is my favourite game of all time. Up there alongside The Witcher 3, Dark Souls 3, Sekiro: Shadows Die Twice, and many more.
